Estimating building material recycling value

On basis of the building materials within a building and some provided building characteristics. The value of various materials within a building on the market is estimated.

  • Easy-to-use.
  • No experts are needed.
  • If the materials are unknown, an estimation can be made.
  • Easily check the recycling value of multiple buildings in a short period of time.
